Amazon’s employee turnover rate is approximately 150%, which costs the company an estimated $8 billion annually. In order to investigate the root causes of high turnover rate, employee sentiment analysis was conducted using 140 reviews of current and former employees on Glassdoor, a platform which allows employees to leave anonymous reviews. I then created data visualizations of the Glassdoor reviews to understand the distribution of positive, negative, and neutral sentiment among current and former employees.
